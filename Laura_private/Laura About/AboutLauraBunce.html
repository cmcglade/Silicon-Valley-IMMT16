About Laura Bunce

Laura is a design enthusiast and a world traveler. Her love for design started at a young age but grew throughout her environmental engineering degree at the University of Guelph. Her interest in both technology and arts have brought her to Centennial College to peruse postgraduate studies in Interactive Media Management.
